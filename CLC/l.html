Library

The library of Cassel Academy was a stately antique building with marble columns supporting elegant arches, topped by parquet glass windows that could see the starry sky, and heavy bookshelves dividing the huge space. In the basement of the library, the huge central mainframe is housed, from the first basement to the sixth basement.
