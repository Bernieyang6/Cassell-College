Ice House

The Cassel Academy's warehouse is named after the low temperature it maintains at all times. Only dangerous items that are out of control are eligible for a place in the Ice Cellar, such as dragon scales, octave boxes with the Emperor's words, alchemical equipment, and dragon bone crosses, with the level of collection increasing the further down you go!
